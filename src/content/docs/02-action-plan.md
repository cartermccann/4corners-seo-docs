## Review Generation Campaign

Reviews are critical for local SEO. Here's the plan:

**Target: 30+ Google Reviews in 90 Days**

**How We Get Reviews**
1. Post-project email sequence requesting reviews
2. Text message follow-up with direct review link
3. QR code on business cards linking to review page
4. Personal request from installers at project completion

**Multi-Platform Strategy**
- Google (priority - affects local rankings)
- Facebook
- Yelp
- HomeAdvisor
- Houzz

**Review Response Protocol**
- Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours
- Thank positive reviewers specifically
- Address negative reviews professionally
- Show future customers you care
