# New Pages We're Creating

> **Each new page targets specific searches that your competitors are ranking for - and you're not.**

---

## Why New Pages Matter

Your current website is missing pages for some of your most searchable services. When someone searches "pool deck coatings Colorado," you don't show up because you don't have a page about pool decks.

**The fix is simple:** Create dedicated pages for each service and each city you serve.

---

## New Service Pages

### 1. Patio Coatings Page

**Target Keyword:** "concrete patio coatings" (1,000 searches/month)

**What This Page Covers:**
- Benefits of coating your patio
- Best coating options for outdoor use
- Colorado climate considerations
- Before/after transformation photos
- Maintenance tips for outdoor coatings
- FAQ section

**Why It Matters:**
This keyword gets 1,000 searches monthly with low competition. Creating this page could bring 50-100 new visitors per month once it ranks.

---

### 2. Pool Deck Coatings Page

## What Each Page Includes

Every new page follows this structure for maximum SEO impact:

### Standard Elements

| Element | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Optimized Title Tag** | Targets primary keyword |
| **Meta Description** | Compelling 155-character summary |
| **H1 Heading** | Clear, keyword-rich page title |
| **500-1,500 Words** | Comprehensive content |
| **Before/After Images** | Visual proof of quality |
| **Customer Testimonials** | Social proof |
| **FAQ Section** | Captures additional searches |
| **Clear CTA** | "Get Your Free Estimate" |

### Technical Elements

| Element | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Schema Markup** | Service or LocalBusiness schema |
| **Internal Links** | Connected to related pages |
| **Image Alt Tags** | Optimized for image search |
| **Canonical Tag** | Prevents duplicate content issues |
| **Fast Load Time** | Under 3 seconds |

## How This Connects to Your Current Site

These new pages integrate with your existing site:

**From Homepage:**
- Service dropdown links to all service pages
- Location section links to city pages
- "Our Services" section showcases new pages

**From Service Pages:**
- Cross-links between related services
- Links to relevant blog posts
- Links to location pages

**From Blog Posts:**
- Every blog links to relevant service pages
- Geographic content links to location pages

This internal linking structure tells Google which pages are most important and how they relate to each other.
