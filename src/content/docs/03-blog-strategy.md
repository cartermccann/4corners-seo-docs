## Blog Post Structure

Every blog post follows this proven format:

### 1. Hook Introduction (100-150 words)
- Start with a problem or question
- Show you understand the reader's situation
- Preview what they'll learn

### 2. Quick Answer Box
- 2-3 sentence direct answer
- Captures featured snippet position
- Satisfies impatient readers

### 3. Detailed Sections (H2 headers)
- Break topic into logical sections
- Use clear, scannable headings
- Include relevant keywords naturally

### 4. Colorado-Specific Content
- Reference local climate challenges
- Mention specific Colorado cities
- Use local examples and photos

### 5. Comparison Tables (when applicable)
- Easy to scan information
- Google loves table content
- Helps readers make decisions

### 6. FAQ Section
- 5-8 related questions
- Captures "People Also Ask" results
- Uses FAQ schema markup

### 7. Call-to-Action
- Clear next step for reader
- Link to relevant service page
- Offer free estimate

## Content Guidelines

### Tone & Voice
- **Helpful** - Focus on solving problems
- **Expert** - Show your knowledge
- **Approachable** - No jargon, plain English
- **Local** - Feel like a Colorado company

### SEO Requirements
- Primary keyword in title and first paragraph
- Keyword in at least one H2 heading
- Related keywords throughout (natural, not forced)
- Internal links to service pages
- External links to authoritative sources
- Optimized images with alt text

### Visual Elements
- Hero image at top
- Before/after photos when relevant
- Infographics for complex topics
- Process photos showing your work
- Color swatch images for coating options
